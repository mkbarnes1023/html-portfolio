Best Movies According to Kenzie

My Top Movies of All Time

Jurassic Park

Steven Spielberg's use of super realistic animatronics & suits for the dinosaurs still holds up to this day, even to the newer movies with CGI.

Wicked

Even though they've only released the first part, the songs and compositions hold up to the musical so far.

Jurassic World: Dominion

Again, can't go wrong with dinosaurs, and the original cast being a critical part in the movie brings back such good memories of where it all started. Alan Grant's final "I told you so".
